About US

Students for Exploration & Development of Space (SEDS)

Who are we?
SEDS BPHC is the rocketry and aerospace club of BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus. It was established in April 2019. The team is presided by a Faculty-in-Charge (Prof. Kannan Ramaswamy, Department of Physics). The team consists of undergraduate students from various disciplines including Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, Biology, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Physics, etc.

What we do?
We participate in two major competitions, the Spaceport America Cup (SA Cup) and the Cansat competition organised by American Astronautical Society(AAS). We’re also working on building our own cube satellites. Not only that, but we also have our own R&D division.

  • Spaceport America Cup

    Our rocketry team’s goal is to build a rocket 8 feet long, weighing approximately 28kg, which flies upto 10,000 feet. The following subsystems work together to build our rocket, Apeiron: Avionics, propulsion, structures, recovery(energetics), biological payload. We have the Operations and Outreach subdivisions as well, which are common to all projects

  • Team Janus: Cansat

    Team Janus is one of the teams representing our campus in the annual international CanSat competition organized by the American Astronautical Society(AAS). At Team Janus, students mainly work in these subsystems: Recovery, structures, avionics, simulations, payload. The payload for the CanSat competition changes every year.

  • NEMO & NEMOLITE : Cubesat

    Cubesats are small, highly sophisticated and intricate satellites that have a multitude of applications like communication, earth observation and defense.We’re looking to launch two satellites, NEMOlite, a 1U demo sat and NEMO, a 2U remote sensing satellite in 2022 and 2024 respectively.
